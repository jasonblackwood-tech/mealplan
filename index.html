STEP 3: My Meal Plan Nutrients

“Limits” are for nutrients we should try to not go too high on every day (like added sugar and saturated fat). “UL” means the "upper limit" or MOST we should probably have each day. Going above the limit every day can have consequences for our health in the long term such as issues with our digestive system, bones, and nervous system, as well as other medical issues like diabetes, heart disease, or stroke.
